# LeanMe Codex

LeanMe is not a software house.

LeanMe builds Hybrid Companies.

Every digital product must combine:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Human Intelligence
- Automation
- Design
- Communication
- Healthcare expertise
- Business processes

Core principles

1. Simplicity
2. Elegance
3. Scalability
4. Automation first
5. Human centered
6. Modular architecture
7. Production ready
8. Long-term maintainability

Every feature must solve a real business problem.

The website is not a showcase.

It is the operating system of LeanMe.

Every page must generate trust, authority and innovation.

The website will continuously evolve and become the entry point of the entire LeanMe ecosystem.
